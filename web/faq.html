Wellcome to HACHI Proyect Manager®

HACHI is the platform where you can create, manage and colaborate on projects. Make your own project and invite your team to help you!

How do I sign up?

In the main page you can register an user account to Log in to HACHI. Fill in all form required fields with your personal data, then press the “Create account” button. If something is wrong with your data, HACHI will notify you right there.

How do I log in?

In the same page you can enter to HACHI using a pre-registered acount. Fill in all form required fields with your acount data, then press the “Log in” button. If something is wrong with your acount, HACHI will notify you right there.

How do I create a project?

Enter the project page and click the "create project" button.

It will pop a form that you will have to fill in with the project details you want. When you are done, click the "Create new" button.

How do I create a task?

Enter the detailed project page and click the "create new task" button situated in the Hive section.

It will pop a form that you will have to fill in with the task details you want. When you are done, click the "Create new" button.

How do I create a post?

Enter the detailed project page and click the "Post a new comment" button situated in the Posts section.

It will pop a form that you will have to fill in with your question or comment. When you are done, click the "Add" button.

How do I add a new member to my project?

Enter the detailed project page and click the "Add new member" button situated in the Members section.

It will pop a form that you will have to fill in with the email or username of the person you want to add. When you are done, click the "Add" button.

How do I join a project?

Enter the projects page and click the "Join" button situated in the upper section.

Enter the access code that gave you the project administrator. The code is situated at the top of the project page.

How do I mark a task as completed?

Enter the detailed task page. You can see the title of the task, the description and due date. You can also leave a comment if you want.

If you wish to mark your assigned task as completed, click the symbol on the upper right section of the page.

How do I delete my account?

If you wish to delete your account, enter to your profile and go to the bottom. You will see the information you'll have to provide to delete your account.
